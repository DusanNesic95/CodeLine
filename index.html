Conversity

Cross-platform embeddable support chat solution.

Conversity is Indian based company, providing embeddable support chat solutions for any platform. App consists of browser based app, which clients can embed to their website via iframe. Second part of the system is a hybrid mobile app, built for Android, iOS and Windows Phone. Third part is admin panel, from where technicians can respond to users problems.

To see the app in detail, check Conversity.net website.

  • Date: July 2016
  • Client: Conversity.net
  • Category: Ionic development

Chakhna

Chakhna is a hybrid app made primarily for Android. The goal of the app is to allow users to find cheap and secure way to deliver their goods where they want. On the other hand users who want to deliver have a chance to quickly earn some extra cash.

  • Client: Tecnosystem
  • Category: Ionic development

Management app for Indian based company IndoCool.
